This is Our About Us Page

CapacityBay is the practical and hands-on eLearning platform and organization established to revolutionize learning and workforce development training for U. S. Veterans, transitioning service members, New Americans, immigrants, refugees as well as others in areas of substantial unemployment and technology deprived communities such as sub-Sahara Africa and other parts of the world. We also provide integrated IT consulting services for individuals, businesses and government agencies. CapacityBay, Inc. began in 2014 with the vision of its founder Obi Nwachukwu to empower individuals and communities by simplifying and making easy how learning online and hands-on from the comfort of your home.
